CARDIO AND MUSIC GOAL-WEIGHT LOSS

If your are trying to loss weight, Cardio is your answer

Cardio exercise increases the ability of the muscles to extract oxygen from the blood. This means the heart does not need to work as hard to pump blood to the muscles. When combined with strength training (such as lifting weights), cardio exercise helps with the prevention and management of heart disease and can also contribute to weight loss. Cardio exercise can contribute to weight loss in several different ways, including increasing calorie expenditure (how many calories your body burns), boosting metabolismby increasing muscle mass, regulating appetite and reducing stress,

Yoga can work wonders for your mental health

Yoga can help reduce stress because it promotes relaxation, which is the natural opposite of stress. Yoga can benefit three aspects of ourselves that are often affected by stress: our body, mind, and breathing. You don't have to wait to feel stressed out to do yoga, and you shouldn't!
